How it works

Numbers from code. Words from AI. Never the reverse.

Most AI "data analysis" hallucinates statistics. Plotline inverts the pipeline: statistics are computed first, deterministically — the AI is only allowed to narrate them.

01

The fact ledger

Regression trends, changepoint detection, MAD outliers, Pearson correlations, streaks, seasonality, category leaders, concentration — computed in pure TypeScript, ranked by importance.

02

The audited narrative

An LLM turns facts into a story arc — then every number in its prose is audited against the ledger. Any scene that fails the audit is rewritten deterministically. Zero hallucinated figures.

03

The cinematic page

A self-contained scrollytelling HTML page: animated charts that draw themselves, a count-up hero stat, three visual themes — and the full fact ledger as an appendix. No trackers, no CDNs.

The honesty model

Built for a world where agents cite agents.

When an agent presents analysis to a human — or to another agent — the numbers must be right. Plotline's architecture makes hallucinated statistics structurally impossible.

Facts before prose

The statistics engine runs first and produces an immutable fact ledger. The narrative layer never sees raw data — only verified facts.

The numeric audit

Every number in AI-written text is extracted and checked against the ledger (0.05 absolute / 1.5% relative tolerance). Failing scenes are replaced with deterministic prose.

Show your work

Every story page ends with its fact ledger: each claim, its computed value, and the method used. Readers — and agents — can verify everything.
